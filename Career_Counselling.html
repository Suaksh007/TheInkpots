Career Counselling

Equip your students with the right tools for career success. Our Career Counselling program offers personalized guidance, helping students explore career paths, build resumes, and prepare for future job opportunities.

What’s Included

  • Personalized one-on-one career Counselling sessions
  • Workshops on resume building, interview preparation, and professional networking
  • Career assessments to match student interests with suitable career paths
  • Guidance on pursuing higher education or entering the workforce

Delivery Model

Our Career Counselling program includes both group workshops and individualized Counselling sessions. It can be tailored for students at different stages — high school, pre-university, or college level — and is available both online and in-person.

Who Should Buy This?

Perfect for schools looking to equip their students with the skills and clarity needed to navigate future career paths confidently, especially institutions preparing students for higher education or the workforce.
